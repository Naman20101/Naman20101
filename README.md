### About

Hey, I'm Naman. 👋
So if y'all are wondering who this guy is, then lemme tell u, I’m 16, in Grade 12, and currently based in Dubai. I don’t call myself an engineer yet—I’m a builder and a student who’s obsessed with how things work.

I spend most of my time exploring STEM and figuring out how to solve real-world problems using tech. Right now, I’m deep-diving into the world of LLMs—not just prompting them, but understanding the mechanics of fine-tuning, weights, and how these models actually "think."

* What I’m focused on:
- LLM Internals: Getting my hands dirty with fine-tuning and model architecture.

- Problem Solving: Building tools that actually fix things, not just "cool" demos.

- Constant Learning: If I don't know how it works, I break it until I do.

*** I’m just a curious guy who likes to build fast and learn faster.

I design, architect, and ship **AI-native, full-stack systems** that tackle problems most people assume require decades or billions to fix—three live systems in production. No co-founder. Every architectural decision — what gets encrypted, what gets logged, when a human overrides the machine — is mine. I move fast using every tool available, and I build things that run.
